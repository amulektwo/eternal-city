## Models — `assets/models/` (THE FORGE)

Three hero models, generated by the Seer in **Meshy AI** (his own paid-account
generations — outputs owned by the account holder per Meshy's terms of service)
and deposited through DROPBOX_FOR_THE_CITY on 2026-07-04. Refined in-house
(gltf-transform + meshoptimizer: weld → simplify to the slot tri budget →
base-center origin → city-matched PBR dress → Draco). The raw generations were
geometry-only (no UVs/textures); the materials in these files are the city's
own factors, not Meshy output.

| File | Source generation | Tris (raw → refined) |
|---|---|---|
| `models/throne.glb` | `Meshy_AI_ornate_gothic_throne__0704144159_generate.glb` | 543,200 → 19,986 |
| `models/fountain.glb` | `Meshy_AI_tiered_white_marble_f_0704144005_generate.glb` | 660,066 → 24,994 |
| `models/gateArch.glb` | `Meshy_AI_grand_gothic_cathedra_0704143946_generate.glb` | 1,859,582 → 9,992 (re-refined from the preserved original in Ascent Pass 3 — 12 instances made the 19,866 version the worst-case tris driver under Law 28) |

Originals preserved in the iCloud dropbox `_PROCESSED/` folder.
