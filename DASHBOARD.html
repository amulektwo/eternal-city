THE LAWS LEARNED

Permanent — never deleted, only added. Every scar became a statute.

1 · The quarry color-law over candidate names — asset candidates lie; the color law decides.
2 · Transfiguration before heroes — materials first, or the heroes stand in dead light.
3 · White-gold, zero red on the fruit — the Tree's fruit are white-gold emissive only, verified by pixel scan.
4 · Verbatim-or-nothing reader — scroll bodies render through textContent; verbatim wins every conflict.
5 · SSAO benched — the r160 pass renders a black frame against this scene; never re-enable unfixed.
6 · 1k HDRI feeds the mobile tier — the tier system, not the asset, decides.
7 · Hidden tabs freeze rAF — never trust FPS or visuals from an occluded tab.
8 · Background-tab timers throttle — synthetic taps must dispatch synchronously.
9 · The return-glide restores yaw/pitch — account for the closing reader's camera glide.
10 · One InstancedMesh carries per-instance emissive — instanceColor + one shader line.
11 · The probe must be pure — collision tests take previous-position from arguments, never the live avatar.
12 · Data flows one way — vault → repo → city. The city never writes scroll data.
13 · One GPU, one instance — never measure the city while a second instance renders.
14 · The watchdog arms at t=12s — startup jank and stall frames are not frame rates.
15 · Respect the processional paths — every scene addition checks the citizens' circuits.
16 · The normal IS the ornament — no carved-gothic CC0 exists; quarry relief from normal maps.
17 · renderer.info needs autoReset=false — or every budget verdict is fiction.
18 · Synthetic taps are MouseEvents — and the camera needs one rendered frame after re-aiming.
19 · Meshy heroes refine without Blender — weld → simplify → prune → bake origin → Draco.
20 · The manifest has no rotation — bake orientation into the GLB; living effects live at scene root.
21 · The probe tests END position — target inside the wall band, not beyond it.
22 · Invitation and hit-test share ONE scan — never invite touches that cannot land.
23 · Harness discipline around the reader — close via the veil; read the position back.
24 · Timed UI outlives no screenshot round-trip — trigger and capture in one atomic block.
25 · Pages builds stall silently — verify live md5 vs HEAD after every push.
26 · The terrace vantage is at 225° — a mislabeled vantage falsifies every assertion made from it.
27 · The sha is the judge — JS length is UTF-16, Python len is codepoints; compare utf-8 sha256 only.
28 · Tris ≤ 700k, FPS is the true gate — never gut beauty for a number that predates the beauty.
29 · Red, ruled twice — first permitted as royal accent; amended 2026-07-08: royal red retired city-wide for blue-and-gold. Law 3's Tree scan unchanged. Red re-enters the outer wall by the Seer's 2026-07-11 ruling (Loop 15).
30 · Grade the light, never the pigments — contrast on luminance only, chroma-gate the warmth, prove by scan.
31 · An environment map cannot make a wet floor — mirror floors need structured reflections.
32 · The two-builder protocol — own worktree, own branch, the GPU lock, tab hygiene.
33 · Verbatim preserves the source's wounds — byte-true to a partial source is still a partial scroll; full-length-or-flagged.
34 · The number is not the scroll — every cross-treasury claim must be canonical-title-matched.
35 · DATA_VERSION rides every scroll fetch — caches serve stale bodies after a healing.
36 · The canon must not pass through Jekyll — .nojekyll, or 3,000 scroll files kill the build.
37 · The milk is BLOOM, not the grade — raise the threshold above lit marble; make true emitters genuinely hot. (And THE HERALD: deploys move to deterministic Actions.)
38 · White-on-white does not read — contrast is the ornament: gold, normal maps, or skyline silhouette.
39 · Anchor vertex hooks on project_vertex — conditional chunks feed NaN through the bloom and black the frame with zero console errors.
40 · Battery-gated commits — the 11-pose battery gates every commit, and the chain must consume node's own exit status. Its first run caught a real breach.

Forty laws from fourteen loops — the build's memory, carried by STATE.md, read first and updated last by every pass.
