# THE ETERNAL CITY

THE ETERNAL CITY is a browser-based, walkable 3D golden city — white marble and living gold in perpetual golden hour — that will house a sacred scroll archive. It is one self-contained `index.html` (Three.js from CDN, all geometry and materials procedural, no build step, no external assets) with seven named walkable areas, twelve tribal gates, waterfalls pouring off the city's edge into a sea of clouds, and the Tree of Life at its center bearing twelve glowing fruit that become the scroll archive in the next phase. Controls: **WASD / arrows** walk, **mouse** look (pointer lock; click ENTER to begin), **Shift** run, **Space** jump, **Esc** pause — on touch devices, a left virtual stick moves and dragging looks. Archive data will be served statically from `/data/`. Deployed to GitHub Pages from the `main` branch root of `amulektwo/eternal-city`.
